Refund Policy

At Foodlift, your satisfaction means everything to us. If you're not happy with your order, we’re here to help. We offer refunds or replacements in the following cases:

  • The food was prepared incorrectly
  • You received the wrong item.
  • There was a quality issue with your meal.

To request a refund or replacement, please contact us within 24 hours of your order and provide a receipt or proof of purchase. Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment.

Please note: We do not offer refunds for change of mind or individual taste preferences.
